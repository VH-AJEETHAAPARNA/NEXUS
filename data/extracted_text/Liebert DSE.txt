The Liebert DSE 400-500kW solution the world's most reliable and efficient water-free 
cooling system for colocation, cloud hosting and other large data centers.  
Offering superior reliability, the Liebert DSE uses proven pumped refrigerant 
economization technology from Vertiv, deployed in more than 6000 installations 
worldwide. It integrates the evaporator, condenser, compressors, refrigerant economizer 
pumps and thermal controls in a single, easy to deploy outdoor packaged solution.  
The highly scalable system is available in rooftop and outdoor perimeter configurations.
